# NEST-Pakistan

**NEST-Pakistan** (Nexus of Energy, Systems and Transitions) is a multi-sector modelling framework for Pakistan that examines the interlinkages between **Energy**, **Water**, and **Agriculture** in the context of climate change and sustainable development.

The framework supports the study *Decarbonization Pathways and Equity Implications of Pakistan's Climate Ambition* and future nexus research on Pakistan's climate transitions.

---

## The Water–Energy–Agriculture Nexus

Pakistan faces deeply interlinked challenges across its energy, water, and agricultural systems. Energy production depends on water (hydropower, cooling), agriculture depends on energy (irrigation pumping, fertiliser) and water, and water management depends on energy. These feedbacks are central to any credible assessment of Pakistan's climate ambition.

NEST-Pakistan models each pillar with dedicated tools and datasets, with a shared scenario framework that allows cross-sectoral analysis.
