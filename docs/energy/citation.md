> Yaseen, A., Awais, M., Niazi, H., Bint Khalil, J., Manzoor, T., & Muhammad, A. (2025). *Decarbonization Pathways and Equity Implications of Pakistan's Climate Ambition*. Centre for Water Informatics & Technology (WIT), LUMS.

## Upstream frameworks

MESSAGEix-Pakistan is built on the `message_ix` and `ixmp` framework. Please also cite:

> Huppmann, D., Gidden, M., Fricko, O., Kolp, P., Orthofer, C., Pimmer, M., Kushin, N., Vinca, A., Mastrucci, A., Riahi, K., & Fricko, O. (2019). The MESSAGEix Integrated Assessment Model and the ix modeling platform (ixmp): An open framework for integrated and cross-cutting analysis of energy, climate, the environment, and sustainable development. *Environmental Modelling & Software*, 112, 143–156. <https://doi.org/10.1016/j.envsoft.2018.11.012>

GAMS must also be acknowledged when reporting results from solved scenarios. See <https://www.gams.com/> for citation guidance.

## Inherited code

The `report/legacy/` and `data/legacy/` directories contain code and configuration inherited from the MESSAGEix-GLOBIOM ENGAGE project (SSP2 v4.1.7). Please acknowledge the ENGAGE project when using the reporting pipeline.
