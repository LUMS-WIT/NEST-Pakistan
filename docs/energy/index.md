# Energy — MESSAGEix-Pakistan

MESSAGEix-Pakistan is a linear energy system optimisation model for Pakistan built on the [`message_ix`](https://docs.messageix.org) and [`ixmp`](https://docs.messageix.org/projects/ixmp) framework maintained by IIASA. It represents Pakistan's energy supply and demand sectors across multiple time periods and optimises least-cost capacity expansion and dispatch subject to policy constraints.

Four scenarios are developed: Current Measures (CM), NDC-Unconditional (NDC-U), NDC-Conditional (NDC-C), and Net Zero (NZ).
