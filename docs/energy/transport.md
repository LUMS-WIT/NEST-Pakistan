# Transport

Transport is Pakistan's second-largest energy-consuming sector and a growing source of CO₂ and air pollutant emissions. It is dominated by road transport, with rail playing a smaller but potentially significant role under decarbonisation scenarios.

## Sector overview

Pakistan's transport energy demand is met almost entirely by oil products (petrol, diesel, CNG for road; jet fuel for aviation). Electrification of road transport is a key lever in the NDC and Net Zero scenarios.

| Sub-sector | Primary fuel (base year) | Share of transport energy |
|---|---|---|
| Road — passenger | Petrol, CNG | — |
| Road — freight | Diesel | — |
| Rail | Diesel, electricity | — |
| Aviation | Jet fuel | — |
